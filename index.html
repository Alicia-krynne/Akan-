AKAN NAMING SYSTEM

Like many african communities, naming children is not done randomly.Names are chosen arcording to gender, time of birth, place of birth or basically anything that can influence

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
